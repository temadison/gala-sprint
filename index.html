Event

Framing the Future Gala • Saturday, April 25, 2015

VIP event begins at 5:30; doors open to all ticketed guests at 6:30
Dock5 at Union Market • 1309 5th St NE

Please join us for an evening of live music and local fare, art and auction, all in support of the arts program for the students at Two Rivers Public Charter School.
Now in its 11th year, Two Rivers’ annual Gala typically brings in over 10% of the arts budget in one night.

Services

  • Art Auction: A variety of exciting art pieces will be available for silent auction during the event. Items include paintings, drawings, sculpture and jewelry from professional artists.
  • Goods & Services Auction: Non-art items available at the auction include amazing restaurants, valuable services and delicious wines.
  • Raffle Drawing: Five opportunities to win big! As prizes are finalized they will be listed below. Drawing occurs the night of the Gala (you do not need to be present to win).
  • Taste Local Restaurant, Brewery & Wine Selections: Some of DCs finest purveyors of food and beverage will be serving up appetizers and delicious beer, wine and cocktails.
  • Musical Entertainment: Dance the night away to tunes played by live musicians and a DJ.
